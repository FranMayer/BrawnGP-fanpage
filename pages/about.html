¿Who are we?

We are passionate about Formula 1 and we pay tribute to the legendary Brawn GP, ​​the team that revolutionized the sport in 2009. We celebrate their innovative spirit, dedication and success, sharing stories and memories of a unique season that marked a before and after in history of racing.


Beginning of a dream...

We are a tribute to the spirit of improvement that defined Brawn GP, ​​the team that surprised the world of Formula 1 in 2009. Born from the remains of Honda Racing, this team began with uncertainty and limited resources, but thanks to the leadership of Ross Brawn and a revolutionary technical approach, they managed to win both championships in their only season. We celebrate that story of effort, ingenuity and passion that inspires all motorsport lovers.


The serie

The series Brawn: The Impossible Formula 1 Story will tell how Ross Brawn saved the former Honda team after the Japanese manufacturer decided to leave F1. It includes interviews with several protagonists, such as Brawn himself, drivers Jenson Button and Rubens Barrichello, technical director Nick Fry and Christian Horner, from Red Bull, Brawn GP's main rival in 2009. Disney also promises behind-the-scenes footage and access to F1 archives, "many of them unpublished." Hollywood star Keanu Reeves presents the documentary and is also one of its executive producers alongside three-time BAFTA winner Neil Duncanson. The series about Brawn GP is produced by North One, written by showrunner Simon Hammerson and directed by Daryl Goodrich, who also previously directed the acclaimed 2017 Ferrari documentary "Ferrari: Race to Immortality."
